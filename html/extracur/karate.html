Karate

Self-defense opens up a realm of freedom for women in an imperfect world. By building confidence in physical resistance skills, women can feel more empowered and secure in potentially dangerous situations, knowing they possess the proper techniques to fend off attackers.
Recognizing the importance of self-defense, I joined the Golden Tiger Karate Training Institute at the age of 9. After three years of dedicated training, I achieved the coveted Black Belt at the age of 12, under the esteemed International Karate Budokan.
In addition, I actively participate in the UAE Taekwondo and Karate Federation, where I've had the privilege of learning from 1st Dan & 2nd Dan Masters, honing my skills and mastering new techniques. I have also been fortunate to attend various martial arts training camps organized by the Federation.
My dedication to martial arts led me to participate in the National Karate Championships, where I secured victory and earned Winner Cups and Certificates in three categories, proudly representing the UAE Taekwondo and Karate Federation in the years 2016 & 2017.
