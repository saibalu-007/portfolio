Dancing

Since childhood, my passion for Art and Culture has been unwavering. I devoted two years to learning Bharata Natyam, a classical dance form. Through active participation in various dancing events, I earned accolades in numerous cultural festivals, stage programs, and exhibitions organized by Indian Clubs and Global Village Organizations in Dubai, UAE. The joy of expressing myself through dance and engaging with diverse audiences has been a truly enriching experience.
